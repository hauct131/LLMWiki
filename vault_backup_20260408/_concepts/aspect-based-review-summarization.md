## Definition

Aspect-Based Review Summarization refers to the process of extracting specific aspects or features mentioned in customer reviews and summarizing opinions related to those particular elements rather than providing a general sentiment score. This technique focuses on granular insights into what customers appreciate or dislike about different attributes of a product or service.

## Why It Matters

It enables businesses to understand detailed feedback, allowing for targeted improvements and more personalized customer experiences based on specific aspects they value most in their products or services.
