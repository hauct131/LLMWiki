## Definition

Aspect-Level Sentiment Classification refers to the process of identifying specific attributes or components (aspects) within a text and determining the sentiments expressed towards those aspects. It goes beyond general opinion by dissecting opinions into finer granularity, such as sentiment directed at particular features like "battery life" in electronic product reviews.

## Why It Matters

Aspect-Level Sentiment Classification enables businesses and researchers to understand consumer preferences with precision, facilitating targeted improvements and personalized marketing strategies.
