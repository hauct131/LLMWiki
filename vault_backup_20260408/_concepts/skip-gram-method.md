## Definition

The Skip-gram method is a neural network architecture used in natural language processing (NLP) to learn word embedd extrinsic representations by predicting context words given a target word. It involves training models like Word2Vec on large text corpora, where each input sample consists of the current word and surrounding context within a certain window size around it.

## Why It Matters

The Skip-gram method is crucial for capturing semantic relationships between words in high dimensional space efficiently.

## Variants / Related Terms

Continuous Bag of Words (CBOW), Hierarchical Softmax, Negative Sampling
