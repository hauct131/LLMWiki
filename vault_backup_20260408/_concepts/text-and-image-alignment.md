## Definition

Text and Image Alignment refers to the process of synchronizing textual content with corresponding visual elements within or across different media types (e.g., images) for effective analysis or processing purposes. This often involves detecting relevant features in both modalities that correspond to each other, such as aligning a specific object depicted in an image with its mention in accompanythyng sentence text.

## Why It Matters

Proper alignment is crucial for accurate sentiment classification and understanding the contextual relationship between multimodal data sources like images and their descriptive texts.
