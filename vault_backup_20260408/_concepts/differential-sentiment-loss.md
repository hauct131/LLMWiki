## Definition

Differential Sentiment Loss refers to a computational approach used in natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning that quantifies the disparity or 'loss' between varying degrees of emotional expressions within text data. This metric helps identify nuances beyond simple positive/negative sentiment classification by considering gradients of sentiments across different contexts.

## Why It Matters

It enables more accurate understanding and processing of complex human emotions in texts, which is crucial for applications like customer feedback analysis or mental health monitoring systems.
