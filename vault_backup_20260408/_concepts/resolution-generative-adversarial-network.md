## Definition

The term 'Resolution Generative Adversarial Network' (GAN) refers specifically to a class within GAN architectures that focuses on the task of image resolution enhancement, wherein networks like ESRGAN are employed for their advanced upscaling capabilities through deep learning. These models use complex convolutional structures with residual connections and dense blocks which allow them not only to learn from low-resolution inputs but also effectively reconstruct high fidelity images that closely resemble the originals in detail, texture, and sharpness without introducing artifacts commonly associated with upscaling methods like interpolation.
