## Definition

Manual Aspect Identification refers to the process where human analysts review text or speech samples to pinpoint specific aspects being discussed within them. This method relies on subjective interpretation and is often used as a benchmark for training automated systems in aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA).

## Why It Matters

It serves as an essential validation tool ensuring that automatic models accurately capture nuanced human sentiments about different aspects of products or services.
