## Definition

Optimize Layers Jointly refers to simultaneously adjusting multiple layers of a neural network during training, with each layer's parameters being updated in consideration of others for improved learning efficiency and performance accuracy. This approach often leads to better feature representation as the interconnected updates promote synergistic improvements across different abstraction levels within the model architecture.
