## Definition

Low Residuals Training is an approach in machine learning where the focus is on minimizing residual errors during model training, particularly within supervised learning frameworks. This technique often involves adjusting network parameters to reduce discrepanency between predicted and actual outputs directly at each iteration of backpropagation.

## Why It Matters

Low Residuals Training can lead to more precise models by continuously correcting errors during the training process, which is crucial for applications requiring high accuracy such as image super-resolution or medical imaging analysis.
