## Definition

Social media post analysis is a process that involves examining content shared on platforms like Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc., to understand user behavior, trends, and sentiments expressed within these digital communications. It often includes the extraction of textual information as well as visual elements such as images or videos associated with posts.

## Why It Matters

Social media post analysis is crucial for businesses seeking insights into consumer preferences and market trends, enabling targeted advertising strategies and brand engagement efforts.
