## Definition

The attention mechanism is a component of neural networks that allows models to focus on specific parts of input data, assigning different levels of importance or 'attention' to various elements. This mimics the human cognitive process where certain stimuli are focused upon more than others when processing information.

## Why It Matters

It significantly improves performance in tasks requiring understanding and generation by providing a dynamic way for models to weigh different parts of data differently, leading to better context comprehension and relevance detection.

## Variants / Related Terms

Soft Attention, Hard Attention (or NONE)
