## Definition

Attention weights are numerical values assigned by a model, typically within neural networks like transformers or attention mechanisms, indicating the importance of different input elements when processing data for specific tasks. These weights help in focusing on relevant parts while diminishing less significant ones during computations such as sequence-to extraneous details extraction.

## Why It Matters

Attention weights are crucial because they enable models to dynamically prioritize information, leading to more accurate and contextually aware outputs for tasks like language translation or image recognition.
