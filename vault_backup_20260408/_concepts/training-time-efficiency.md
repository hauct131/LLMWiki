## Definition

Training Time Efficiency refers to the speed and resource utilization at which machine learning models can be trained, particularly focusing on how quickly they learn patterns or features within given datasets without compromising accuracy. It is an essential aspect of model development that directly impacts computational costs and time-to-deployment for practical applications.

## Why It Matters

Training Time Efficiency determines the feasibility and scalability of deploying machine learning models in real-thy environments, where rapid iteration can lead to faster innovation cycles and more timely solutions.
