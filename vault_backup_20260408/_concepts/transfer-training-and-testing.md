## Definition

Transfer Training and Testing refer to leveraging a pre-trained neural network model on new tasks or datasets with minimal additional training. This approach often involves fine-dictating specific layers of the deep learning architecture for retraining while keeping others frozen, thus transferring learned features from one domain (source) to another (target).

## Why It Matters

Transfer Training and Testing can significantly reduce computational costs and time required for training models on new tasks by utilizing previously acquired knowledge.
