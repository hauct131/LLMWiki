## Definition

Aspect-oriented Method (AoM) is an approach in software engineering that integrates aspect-oriented programming principles with traditional object-oriented paradigms, focusing on crosscutting concerns and promoting modularity by separating them into distinct aspects. This methodology enhances code maintainability and reusability through the encapsulation of behaviors affecting multiple classes or objects in a single module called an aspect.

## Why It Matters

AoM is significant for developing complex software systems as it facilitthy efficient handling of concerns that cut across various parts of the system, leading to cleaner and more organized codebases.
