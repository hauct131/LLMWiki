## Definition

ELMo (Embeddlaus Recurrent Neural Network) and GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) are deep learning-based natural language processing models that generate word embeddings contextually. Unlike traditional static word vectors, these dynamic representations capture the complexity of linguistic semantics by considering both syntactic and semantic aspects within a given textual input.

## Why It Matters

ELMo and GPT significantly advance machine understanding of natural language through their deep learning architectures that produce contextually rich embeddings for various NLP tasks such as sentiment analysis, question answering, and translation.

## Variants / Related Terms

Bidirectional Representations from Transformers (BERT), Generative Pre-trained Language Models (GPT) series including GPT-2/3
