## Definition

The Distribution-Aware Sampling Module (DASM) is a component designed within machine learning frameworks that intelligently selects training samples based on their distributional characteristics. It ensures diverse and representative data points are included during model training, aiming to improve the robustness and generalization of learned models by mitigating biases inherent in imbalanced datasets.

## Why It Matters

DASM enhances machine learning algorithms' ability to learn from skewed or incomplete distributions without human intervention for sample selection.
