## Definition

Binary Text Categorization refers to the process of classifying text documents into two distinct categories or classes based on their content. This method simplifies complex classification tasks by reducing them to binary decisions and often employs machine learning algorithms for automated sorting.

## Why It Matters

It is crucial in filtering information efficiently, enabling quick decision-making processes such as spam detection or sentiment analysis on social media platforms.
