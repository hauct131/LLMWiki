## Definition

PN-Polarity Determination refers to the process of classifying text into positive or negative sentiment categories based on the polarity expressed in language use. This involves analyzing words and phrases within documents to ascertain whether they convey a favorable (positive) or unfavorable (negative) attitude towards subjects mentioned therein.

## Why It Matters

Understanding PN-Polarity is crucial for businesses seeking insights into customer opinions, market trends analysis, and social media monitoring to inform strategic decisions.
