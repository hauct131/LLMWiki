## Definition

Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) is an advanced natural language processing technique that focuses on identifying and categorizing opinions expressed in text specifically regarding distinct attributes or "aspects" of a product or service mentioned within. It goes beyond general sentiment analysis by dissecting the nuanced sentiments associated with individual components rather than providing an overall positive, negative, or neutral assessment.

## Why It Matters

ABSA provides businesses and researchers detailed insights into consumer opinions on specific features of their offerings, enabling targeted improvements and personalized marketing strategies.

## Variants / Related Terms

Opinion Target Recognition (OTR), Aspect Extraction (AE)
