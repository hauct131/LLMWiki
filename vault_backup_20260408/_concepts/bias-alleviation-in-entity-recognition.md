## Definition

Bias alleviation in entity recognition refers to methods or techniques applied within named entity recognition (NER) systems aimed at reducing prejudiced outcomes and ens extrinsic biases that may arise from the training data. These approaches ensure a more equitable identification of entities across different demographics, preventing skewed results based on factors like race, gender, or socioeconomic status.

## Why It Matters

It is crucial for maintaining fairness and objectivity in AI systems that process sensitive information from diverse sources such as social media posts.

## Variants / Related Terms

Fair NER (FairNer), Bias-Aware Entity Recognition, Debiased Machine Learning Models
