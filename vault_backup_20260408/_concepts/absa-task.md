## Definition

Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) is a subtask of sentiment analysis that focuses on identifying the specific aspects mentioned in a text and determining the sentiment polarity (positive, negative, or neutral) associated with each aspect. It goes beyond general sentiment to provide fine-grained insights into what particular features or attributes are being praised or criticized within user reviews or comments.

## Why It Matters

ABSA helps businesses understand detailed customer feedback and improve specific areas of their products or services by pinpointing exact aspects that elicit positive or negative sentiments from consumers.
