## Definition

Sparse Data Training refers to machine learning techniques specifically designed to handle datasets where most of the elements are zeros or near-zero. This approach is crucial for efficiently processing and extracting meaningful patterns from such data structures without expending unnecessary computational resources on irrelevant information.

## Why It Matters

It enables effective analysis and predictive modeling in fields like natural language processing, where high dimensionality often leads to sparsity of the input features.

## Variants / Related Terms

Sparse Autoencoders, L1 Regularization (or None)
