## Definition

High Quality Images refer to visual content that has been captured or created with a high level of detail and clarity, often exceeding standard resolutions like Full HD (1080p). These images are characterized by their sharpness, vibrant colors, and minimal noise.

## Why It Matters

High Quality Images enhance the visual fidelity in various applications such as photography, filmmaking, graphic design, and medical imaging, leading to more immersive experiences or accurate diagnoses.

## Variants / Related Terms

Ultra HD (4K), 8K resolution images; HDR (High Dynamic Range) Images
