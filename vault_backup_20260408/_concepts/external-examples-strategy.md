## Definition

External Examples Strategy in machine learning refers to leveraging additional, external datasets or examples that are not part of the primary training set but can provide supplementary information for model improvement and generalization during tasks such as single image super-resolution (SR). This approach often involves using diverse data sources with varying resolutions.

## Why It Matters

It enhances the robustness and accuracy of deep learning models by exposing them to a wider range of scenarios, reducing overfitting on limited training samples.

## Variants / Related Terms

Cross-domain examples, multi-resolution datasets (or NONE)
