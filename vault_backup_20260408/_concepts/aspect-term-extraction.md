## Definition

Aspect Term Extraction involves identifying specific attributes or features mentioned within individual text segments, such as product reviews, that relate directly to particular aspects of the subject being reviewed—like food quality in a restaurant setting. It focuses on pinpointing terms associated with sensory experiences (taste, smell), service, ambiance, and other relevant characteristics expressed by customers.

## Why It Matters

Aspect Term Extraction is crucial for businesses to understand customer feedback at a granular level, enabling targeted improvements in specific areas that directly impact consumer satisfaction.
