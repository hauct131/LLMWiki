## Definition

High Resolution Images refer to photographs or visual content that have been enhanced beyond the original capture resolution, often using digital processing techniques. These images contain more pixels and detail than standard definition (SD) imagery but are not as high-resolution as those captured by professional cameras with advanced optics like DSLRs; they typically represent an intermediate level of image clarity that is higher than what can be perceived natively on most displays.

## Why It Matters

High Resolution Images play a crucial role in various fields such as digital photography, medical imaging, and satellite surveillency by providing more detail for analysis or visual pleasure without the need to capture images at an extremely high resolution with specialized equipment.
