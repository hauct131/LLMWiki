## Definition

The Transformer architecture is an innovative neural network design that relies on self-attention mechanisms to process sequential data, such as natural language, without the need for recurrent or convolutional layers. It consists of multiple identical layers with encoders and decoders stacked together, enabling parallelization during training which significantly speeds up computation compared to RNNs (Recurrent Neural Networks).

## Why It Matters

The Transformer architecture has revolutionized the field of natural language processing by providing a highly efficient model for understanding context and relationships within text.

## Variants / Related Terms

BERT, GPT-2, T5
