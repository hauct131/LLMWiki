## Definition

The No-Aspect Template is a methodological tool used in Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) that involves substituting the aspect term with an unbiased placeholder character within sentences to neutralize sentiment bias, thereby facilitating more objective analysis of opinions related to specific features or aspects.

## Why It Matters

It enables researchers and practitioners in ABSA tasks to systematically remove inherent sentiments from text data for a clearer understanding of the underlying aspect-specific feedback without prejudice.
