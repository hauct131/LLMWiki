## Definition

Syntactic Word Relationships refer to the connections and dependencies between words within sentences, governed by syntactical rules such as grammar and sentence structure. These relationships help determine how different parts of speech interact with each other semantically.

## Why It Matters

Understanding these relationships is crucial for natural language processing tasks like parsing, translation, and sentiment analysis to accurately interpret meaning from text data.
