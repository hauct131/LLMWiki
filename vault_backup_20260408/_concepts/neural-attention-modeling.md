## Definition

Neural Attention Modeling refers to the application of neural networks that incorporate attention mechanisms to focus on specific parts or aspects within a data set. This approach is particularly useful for tasks where different elements contribute unequally, such as understanding which words in a sentence most influence sentiment about an aspect being analyzed.

## Why It Matters

Neural Attention Modeling enhances the accuracy of complex natural language processing (NLP) tasks by enabling models to dynamically focus on relevant information within input sequences.

## Variants / Related Terms

Sequence-todependent attention, Multi-head self-attention
