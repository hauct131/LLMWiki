## Definition

Entity spans identification is a process within multi-modal named entity recognition (MNER) that focuses on pinpointing specific segments or "spans" of text which correspond to entities and classifying these into predefined categories. This task becomes particularly challenging in social media posts where informal language, abbreviations, and multimedia elements are commonplace.

## Why It Matters

Accurate entity spans identification is crucial for effective information extraction from noisy data sources like social media to understand user sentiments or extract factual content efficiently.

## Variants / Related Terms

Coreference resolution (in the context of resolving different mentions referring to the same real-world object), Named Entity Recognition and Disambiguation, Co-reference Resolution in Social Media Contexts; none for specific subtypes not directly related to spans identification itself.
