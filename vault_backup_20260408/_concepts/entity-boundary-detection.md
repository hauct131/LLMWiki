## Definition

Entity boundary detection is a computer vision technique used to identify and locate distinct objects within images or video frames, effectively delineating their edges for further analysis. It involves recognizing specific shapes or patterns that correspond to different entities in the visual data.

## Why It Matters

This process enhances object recognition systems by providing precise localization of items, which is crucial for tasks like image segmentation and scene understanding.

## Variants / Related Terms

Edge detection algorithms (e.g., Sobel, Canny), Region-based methods, Deep learning approaches with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) specialized in boundary delineation such as U-Net or Mask R-CNN.
