## Definition

Objective Scores Calculation refers to the systematic process used in opinion mining (OM) or sentiment analysis where numerical values are assigned to textual data based on their expressed sentiments. These scores typically range from negative to positive and often include a neutral midpoint, reflecting varying degrees of subjectivity within opinions found across documents.

## Why It Matters

Objective Scores Calculation is crucial for quantifying public sentiment in large datasets, enabling businesses and researchers to make data-driven decisions based on the collective opinion trends observed from textual sources like reviews or social media posts.
