## Definition

Semantic analysis of synsets involves examining groups of words or terms that are considered semantically equivalent (synonyms) within lexical databases like WordNet. This process helps in understanding and mapping out relationships between different meanings a word can have, which is essential for various natural language processing tasks such as text comprehension and information retrieval.

## Why It Matters

Semantic analysis of synsets enables more accurate interpretation of meaning beyond simple keyword matching, leading to improved performance in applications like opinion mining and machine translation.

## Variants / Related Terms

Antonymy (opposite meanings), Hyponymy/Hypernymy (subordinate or superordinate relationships)
