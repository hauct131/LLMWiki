## Definition

Overall Polarity Detection refers to computational methods used in sentiment analysis that aim at determining whether a piece of text expresses positive, negative, or neutral sentiments. It assesses the emotional tone behind words to understand attitudes and opinions expressed within it.

## Why It Matters

Overall Polarity Detection is crucial for businesses seeking insights into customer feedback on products and services through social media monitoring and reviews analysis, enabling targeted improvements in service delivery or product development.
