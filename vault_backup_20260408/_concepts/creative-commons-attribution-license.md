## Definition

The Creative Commons Attribution License (CC BY) is a public copyright license that allows for the free distribution of an original work with attribution to its creator while permitting others to distribute it without restriction. It enables sharing, use, and building upon the material legally as long as proper credit is given.

## Why It Matters

This licensse facilitates collaboration and knowledge dissemination in academia and beyond by removing legal barriers while ensuring original authors are recognized for their contributions.

## Variants / Related Terms

Attribution (CC BY), Share-Alike (CC BY-SA)
