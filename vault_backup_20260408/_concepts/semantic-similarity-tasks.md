## Definition

Semantic Similarity Tasks involve evaluating how closely related two pieces of text are in meaning. These tasks typically require algorithms to determine if pairs or sets of words/phrases share similar concepts and contextual usage within a given language framework. The goal is often to quantify the degree of semantic similarity between linguistic items using various computational models.

## Why It Matters

Semantic Similarity Tasks are crucial for developing more intuitive natural language processing systems that can understand human-like nuances in communication and information retrieval applications such as search engines, recommendation systems, or question answering bots.
