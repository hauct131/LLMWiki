## Definition

Interactive Attention Networks are neural network architectures that incorporate attention mechanisms to focus selectively on different parts of input data for tasks such as sentiment analysis at the aspect level, enhancing model performance by learning context-specific representations.

## Why It Matters

They improve accuracy in identifying and classifying sentiments associated with specific aspects within a textual dataset.
