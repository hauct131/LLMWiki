## Definition

Subjective Term Extraction refers to the process within opinion mining where specific terms that express personal opinions or sentiments are identified from text data. This technique focuses on extracting subjective expressions rather than objective facts and categorizes them accordingly for further analysis.

## Why It Matters

It is crucial in understanding public sentiment, customer feedback, and social media trends by distinguishing between factual information and personal opinions within large datasets.

## Variants / Related Terms

Opinion mining (OM), Sentiment Analysis
