## Definition

Interpolation Free Mapping is a technique used within deep learning models for image processing that directly generates high-resolution images from low-resolution inputs without relying on traditional interpolation methods. This approach typically involves neural networks trained specifically to predict and construct finer details in an image, effectively enhancing its resolution by leveraging learned patterns rather than mathematical estimations of intermediate pixels.

## Why It Matters

Interpolation Free Mapping significantly improves the quality of super-resolution images beyond what is achievable with standard interpolation techniques alone.
