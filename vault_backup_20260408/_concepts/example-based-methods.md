## Definition

Example Based Methods in computer vision and machine learning refer to techniques that utilize specific examples or samples of data, such as high-quality reference images, to guide the processing tasks like image super-resolution (SR). These methods often involve matching patches from low-resolution inputs with corresponding areas on higher resolution exemplars.

## Why It Matters

Example Based Methods are crucial for ach0175ing high fidelity in upscaled images by learning detailed textures and patterns directly from examples, leading to more realistic enhancements compared to purely model-based approaches that rely on mathematical transformations.
