## Definition

Social Media Sentiments refer to the collective emotional expressions and opinions expressed on social media platforms through posts, comments, tweets, etc. These sentiments are often analyzed at both macro (overall platform sentiment) and micro levels (individual post or comment). The analysis can be performed using natural language processing techniques that consider linguistic cues as well as contextual information from images, videos, and emojis associated with the content.

## Why It Matters

Understanding social media sentiments is crucial for businesses to gauge public opinion about their products or services, monitor brand reputation, and tailor marketing strategies effectively.

## Variants / Related Terms

Sentiment Analysis (macro), Emoji Sentiment Interpretation, Multimodal Sentiment Fusion
