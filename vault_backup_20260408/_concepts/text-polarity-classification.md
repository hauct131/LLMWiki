## Definition

Text Polarity Classification refers to the automated process of identifying and categorizing opinions expressed in text documents as positive, negative, or neutral. This involves analyzing subjective information within texts using natural language processing (NLP) techniques.

## Why It Matters

It is crucial for businesses seeking customer feedback analysis, social media monitoring, market research, and improving user experience by understanding sentiment trends efficiently.
