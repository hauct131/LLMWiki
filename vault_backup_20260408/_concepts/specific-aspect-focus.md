## Definition

Specific Aspect Focus refers to the concentration on particular attributes or components of a product or service within aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA). This approach dissects sentiments expressed about different aspects rather than providing an overall opinion. It involves identifying and evaluating individual elements that contribute to customer feedback, such as quality, price, or usability.

## Why It Matters

Specific Aspect Focus enables businesses to pinpoint precise areas for improvement by understanding detailed consumer sentiments about distinct features of their offerings.

## Variants / Related Terms

Fine-grained sentiment analysis, Attribute-based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA)
