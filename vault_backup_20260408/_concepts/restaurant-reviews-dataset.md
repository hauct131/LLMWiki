## Definition

A Restaurant Reviews Dataset is a curated collection of text data comprising customer reviews about dining establishments, often annotated for various aspects such as food quality, price level, service experience, etc., to facilitate natural language processing tasks like sentiment analysis and aspect-based evaluation.

## Why It Matters

It serves as a valuable resource for training machine learning models in understanding human sentiments towards specific attributes of restaurants from textual feedback.
