## Definition

The Residual Channel Attention Network (RCAN) is an advanced neural network architecture designed to enhance feature representation by focusing on inter-channel relationships within images through attention mechanisms specifically tailored for residual learning. It employs multiple stages of channel and spatial recalibrations, allowing the model to emphasize important features while suppressing less relevant ones in both channels and spatial dimensions.

## Why It Matters

RCAN significantly improves image super-resolution by effectively capturing fine details that are often lost at lower resolutions or through compression techniques.
