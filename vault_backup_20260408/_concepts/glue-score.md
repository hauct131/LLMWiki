## Definition

The GLUE score is a composite evaluation metric used in natural language processing tasks, which aggregates performance across various datasets and models to assess the overall effectiveness of algorithms at understanding human language nuances such as grammar, context, and sentiment.

## Why It Matters

It provides an objective benchmark for comparing different approaches' abilities to process and interpret text data accurately in natural settings.
