## Definition

Transformer and BERT methods are advanced machine learning techniques primarily used for natural language processing tasks such as aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA). The transformer architecture facilitates parallelization of sequence data processing, while BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) leverages deep bidirectional context to understand the nuances in text.

## Why It Matters

These methods significantly improve the accuracy and efficiency of ABSA by capturing complex language patterns effectively.

## Variants / Related Terms

None, as they refer specifically to distinct models within a broader framework rather than variations or subtypes thereof.
