## Definition

Real-Time Performance Optimization refers to the process of enhancing computational efficiency and speed in executing algorithms or systems so that they can operate within a time frame suitable for immediate feedback or interaction. This often involves refining code, hardware acceleration, parallel processing techniques, and algorithmic adjustments specific to real-time constraints.

## Why It Matters

It is crucial for applications where timely responses are essential, such as in video streaming services, gaming consoles, medical devices monitoring systems, or any interactive technology requiring instantaneous data handling and processing capabilities.
