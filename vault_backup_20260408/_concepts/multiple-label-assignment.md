## Definition

Multiple Label Assignment refers to the process in data processing where each instance can be simultaneously assigned with multiple labels or categories that best describe its characteristics or content accurately. This approach acknowledges the complexity and multifaceted nature of many datasets, especially those involving natural language understanding tasks like sentiment analysis.

## Why It Matters

Multiple Label Assignment is crucial for capturing nuanced information in data classification systems where instances may not fit neatly into a single category but rather belong to several relevant ones simultaneously.
