## Definition

A Multi-Modal Adjacency Matrix represents connections in an emantic graph across different types of data or modalities, such as textual information alongside visual cues. Each entry within this matrix denotes the relationship strength between elements from various modes (e.g., images and their corresponding descriptions).

## Why It Matters

It enables more comprehensive understanding by integrating diverse forms of evidence in data analysis tasks like sentiment classification or image recognition, leading to improved performance over traditional single-modality methods.

## Variants / Related Terms

Weighted adjacency matrix, Unified representation model (or NONE)
