## Definition

Database assessment for Super-Resolution (SR) algorithms refers to evaluating and analyzing collections of data within databases specifically curated with various resolutions. This process involves testing SR methods on these datasets to determine their effectiveness in enhancing image quality by increasing detail beyond the original low-resolution input images.

## Why It Matters

Database assessment is crucial for validating and improving Super-Resolution algorithms, ensdicting higher fidelity restorations of imagery which are essential across numerous applications such as satellite imagery analysis and medical diagnostics.
