## Definition

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) based methods involve a pair of neural networks—the generator and discriminator—competing against each other to generate high-quality synthetic images. The generator creates new data instances that mimic the real ones, while the discriminator evaluates them for authenticity. This process continues until both models reach an equilibrium where the generated images are indistinguishable from actual photographs.

## Why It Matters

GAN-based methods have revolutionized image generation and enhancement tasks by producing highly realistic results that often surpass traditional techniques in quality and detail.

## Variants / Related Terms

Progressive growing of GANs, Conditional GANs (cGANs), CycleGAN for unpaired images translation among others; None if not specifying subtypes within the broad concept.
