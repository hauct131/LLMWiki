## Definition

Image Text Alignment refers to the process of precisely positioning accompanying text over an image in such a way that it corresponds accurately with specific elements or regions within the visual content, often for analysis purposes. This alignment is crucial when dealing with multimodal data where both images and texts are present together but may not be naturally aligned by default due to different sources of origin.

## Why It Matters

Proper text-image alignment enables more accurate extraction of aspects related to sentiment analysis, which can significantly improve the performance of systems analyzing multimodal data like product reviews or social media posts with images and captions.

## Variants / Related Terms

Exact Alignment, Approximate Alignment (or NONE)
