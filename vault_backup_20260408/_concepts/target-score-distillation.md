## Definition

Target Score Distillation (TSD) is a technique used within the context of machine learning, specifically for training neural networks. It employs a loss function known as Target Score Matching (TSM), which aligns the predicted scores from a student model with those of a teacher or more complex model to improve performance and knowledge transfer without direct access to the latter' extrinsic data source(s).

## Why It Matters

TSD enhances neural network training by effectively distilling critical information, leading to better generalization in tasks where labeled data is scarce.
