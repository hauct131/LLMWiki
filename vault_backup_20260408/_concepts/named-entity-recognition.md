## Definition

Named Entity Recognition (NER) is a subtask of information extraction that seeks to locate and classify named entities mentioned in unstructured text into predefined categories such as person names, organizations, locations, dates, and quantities. It involves identifying these specific pieces of data within the context they appear.

## Why It Matters

NER is crucial for organizing large datasets efficiently by extracting key information that can be used in various applications like search engines, question answering systems, and content summarization tools.

## Variants / Related Terms

Person Entity Recognition (PER), Organizational Entity Recognition (ORG), Location Entity Recognition (LOC)
