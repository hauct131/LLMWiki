## Summary

Sentiment analysis seeks to determine overall sentiment in text; SemEval-2014 Task 4 focuses specifically on aspect-based sentiments for target entities like restaurants and laptops. The task provided datasets with manually annotated reviews offering insights into specific aspects such as battery or screen quality, inviting research contributions from various teams worldwide. It resulted in substantial engagement, receiving over a hundred submissions aimed at advancing aspect-based sentiment analysis techniques.

## Key Methodology

- Aspect Term Extraction: Identifying all aspects mentioned within review sentences.
- Aspect Polarity Determination: Assigning sentiment polarities to identified aspects in a given text.
- Dataset Provision for Restaurant and Laptop Reviews: Supplying datasets containing manually annotated reviews of restaurants and laptops respectively, with predefined aspect terms and their sentiments.
- Subtask Selection Flexibility: Allowing participants to choose between different subtasks (SB1 or SB2) based on the domain they are interested in - restaurant or laptop reviews.
- Domain Specificity for Datasets: Providing datasets specifically tailored towards either restaurants or laptops, with aspects and sentiments relevant to each context.
